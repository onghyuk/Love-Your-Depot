    Logo of Team Depot and Love Your Depot, 2019

    Based on the scenes from the process of the exhibition set up, Donghyun designed the logo of Love Your Depot (LUD) and Team Depot by watching them by himself in the field. The LUD logo was designed variable in shape, implying the image of free to stack up and tear down. And the Team Depot logo was designed by reflecting the impression Donghyun got from the structure of the Team Depot office.
