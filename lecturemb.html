Depot Talk : Dr. Caroline Mawer #1

Caroline Mawer was a doctor before becoming an artist in 2017. In the first Depot Talk, she presents the first performance she made using the storytelling devices provided by Jewyo Rhii and Jihyun Jung in the Dawn Breaks workshop, hosted by the Showroom in London. You can see how she has grown as an artist with the piece of work.
